ABOUT

GA Assessing was founded in 2003, by myself Graeme Abbott. I am a member of the IMA in Cape Town and a qualified motor assessor with 30 years experience in the trade. I completed the NCT 2 & 3 at Oude Molen Technical College, the Trade Test as a Motor Mechanic at Faber Nissan in Parow as well as completing the Fitting and Turning Trade Test at Cape Town City Council in Pinelands, Mechanical Branch.

Over the years, I have worked and gained extensive experience in many panel beating workshops.

I compile a full detailed report and take digital photos of all damages both related and old. My turn around time is approximately 24 hours on all motor claims provided the vehicle does not need to be stripped for closer inspection. I cover the whole of the Western Cape, from Vredenburg on the West Coast to Hermanus on the South Coast and all areas in and around Cape Town.
